1. Benefits of a clean mattress

A clean mattress gives you restful sleep. It is free from dirt, dust, and skin flakes that can make you sick. Some people breathe better on a clean mattress without dust. It does not have spills or stains that can smell bad.

A fresh bed also helps to keep your skin healthy. Mites and bugs do not like clean beds so they will stay away too! Steam cleaning is a top pick for keeping mattresses in Singapore spick and span!

2. Health risks of a dirty mattress

A dirty mattress can pose health risks to you and your family. When mattresses are not cleaned regularly, they can become a breeding ground for allergens, bacteria, dust mites, and bed bugs.

These microorganisms can cause allergies, respiratory problems, and skin irritations. Furthermore, an unclean mattress may contribute to poor indoor air quality, leading to worsened asthma symptoms or other respiratory conditions.

Dust mite droppings and mold spores found in dirty mattresses can trigger allergic reactions such as coughing, sneezing, watery eyes, and even asthma attacks. Stains on the mattress also provide a favorable environment for bacteria growth.
